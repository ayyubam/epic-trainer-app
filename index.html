FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

What is EMR Trainer App?
EMR Trainer App is an all-in-one platform for Epic EMR professionals. It includes a hospital directory, training video library, AI-powered tools, quiz and flashcards, SmartPhrase templates, go-live checklists, and more — all designed to help trainers, go-live specialists, and clinical staff work more efficiently.
Do I need an Epic license to use this?
No. EMR Trainer App is an independent training resource platform. You do not need an Epic license, UserWeb access, or any affiliation with Epic Systems to use this app. It's built to complement your Epic training, not replace it.
Can I cancel my subscription anytime?
Yes, absolutely. You can cancel your Pro or Teams subscription at any time with no cancellation fees. You'll retain access through the end of your billing period. We also offer a full refund within 7 days of your initial payment if you're not satisfied.
Is this good for someone new to Epic?
Absolutely. The free plan includes training videos, flashcards, and quizzes that are perfect for beginners. The step-by-step Playground guide walks you through accessing and practicing in Epic's training environment. Many nurses and new hires use it to prepare before their first day.
What AI tools are included in Pro?
Pro includes an AI training assistant (ask any Epic question), SmartPhrase generator, training plan builder, workflow step-by-step generator, Epic error explainer, tip sheet generator, and PDF document analyzer. All powered by Claude AI for accurate, detailed responses.
What's the difference between Pro and Teams?
Pro is designed for individual trainers and specialists. Teams adds multi-user access, shared resources, team training agendas, bulk uploads, and an admin panel — ideal for go-live teams, healthcare IT departments, or training departments with multiple staff members.
